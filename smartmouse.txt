Smart Mouse [Mouse clone]
Huan Le Tao Qi Shu - Smart Mouse (China) (Unl)


Gameplay
The player uses a four-ways buttons and a single button to control Mouse. 
Pressing the button while pushing the four-ways buttons against an 
ice block will cause it to slide in that direction until it hits 
another block or a wall, if the space directly ahead of it is 
unoccupied by a block or wall. 
If that space is occupied, pressing the button will crush the block instead.
