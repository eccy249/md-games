Doroppu

Doroppu is a brand new indie homebrew game for the Sega Genesis / Megadrive. It is a tile matching game with 
influences taken from Puyo Puyo and Puzzle Bobble to create a unique new experience. Doroppu can be played 
by 1 player, or 2 players in split screen vs mode.

Created using BasiEgaXorz, Doroppu is Repixel8’s first of a line of new games for Sega’s 16 bit console.

Doroppu is released as a Rom download, with a limited physical cartridge release following.
