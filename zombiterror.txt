Zombi Terror
Kabuto Factory 
2015 
Text adventure 

A horror text adventure game. Also available on ZX Spectrum, C64, MSX, ZX81, and PC Windows. 


Agosto, 1981. Te dispones a tomar unas merecidas vacaciones cuando recibes un telegrama de tu padre, el doctor Herbert Von Freudstein: 
'El experimento ha salido mal. Por favor, ven lo antes posible.' Preocupado, alquilas un barco rumbo a Isla Almanzora con la intención de ayudar a tu padre...
August, 1981. You're going to take some days for holidays when you receive a short telegram from your father, doctor Herbert Von Freudstein: 
'Experiment has gone bad. Please, come to island asap.' Worried, you rent a boat to Almanzora Island, in order to help your father...
